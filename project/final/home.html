  Our story

2008 Owned by Rogers Communications, Zoocasa.com becomes one of the top real estate search sites in Canada, selling buyer leads to real estate agents across Canada.
2015 CEO Lauren Haw leads the purchase of Zoocasa.com and establishes a new vision: a full service, in-house real estate team supported by modern technology and marketing.
2016 Zoocasa.com unveils a clean and modern design that supports the new vision and future features and functionality.
